About Us

Welcome to Ruchi Tarangam (Waves of Flavors)! We are dedicated to bringing the pure essence of authentic, traditional, homemade culinary delights straight to your doorstep. Every blend, pickle, and sweet is prepared with care, matching the precise recipes passed down through generations. Keeping health and purity at our core, we completely discard bad industry practices: our kitchen functions completely without added artificial preservatives, chemical enhancements, unnatural colorings, or palm oil. We select only first-rate premium raw oils and native ingredients to let you feel the true wave of home-cooked luxury.
